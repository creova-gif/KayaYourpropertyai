# LandlordOS - AI-Powered Rental Management Platform

## Overview
A comprehensive dual-sided platform for Ontario landlords and tenants with AI-powered automation, compliance, and analytics.

## 🎯 Key Features

### Landlord Portal
1. **Dashboard** - Real-time analytics with charts, AI insights, and property overview
2. **Applications** - AI-powered tenant screening with risk scoring (0-100), fraud detection, and swipe-to-approve functionality
3. **Properties** - Multi-property management with unit tracking and occupancy visualization
4. **Tenants** - Complete tenant profiles with payment history and risk assessment
5. **Payments** - Rent collection tracking with late payment alerts and revenue forecasting
6. **Maintenance** - Issue tracking with AI categorization, priority levels, and cost estimates
7. **Documents** - Centralized document hub for leases, notices, receipts, and compliance
8. **Analytics** - Predictive AI insights for revenue forecasting, tenant churn, and occupancy trends
9. **Settings** - Profile management, notifications, LTB compliance, and billing

### Tenant Portal
1. **Home Dashboard** - Lease overview, payment status, and quick actions
2. **Payments** - View payment history, upcoming rent, auto-pay management, and receipts
3. **Documents** - Access lease agreement, receipts, and important documents
4. **Maintenance** - Submit requests with photos, track status, and receive updates

## 🤖 AI Features

### Intelligent Automation
- **Tenant Risk Scoring**: 0-100 score based on credit, income, rental history
- **Fraud Detection**: Automatically validates documents for authenticity
- **Document Verification**: AI checks IDs, pay stubs, bank statements
- **Maintenance Categorization**: Analyzes photos and descriptions to prioritize issues
- **Predictive Analytics**: Forecasts late payments, tenant churn, and revenue
- **AI Assistant**: Contextual help trained on Ontario RTA and LTB decisions

### Ontario LTB Compliance
- Standard lease generation (RTA compliant)
- Automated N4/N5/N12 notice generation
- Rent increase tracking with 90-day notice reminders
- Legal compliance checks

### Technology Stack
- **Framework**: React with React Router 7
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4
- **Charts**: Recharts
- **Animations**: Motion (Framer Motion)
- **Icons**: Lucide React

### Design Principles
- Modern SaaS aesthetic (inspired by Stripe, Airbnb, Notion, Linear)
- Indigo/Purple color scheme with gradient accents
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Smooth animations and micro-interactions
- Clean card-based layouts with soft shadows
