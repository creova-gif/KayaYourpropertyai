. Design Philosophy (Creative but Smooth)

Your platform should feel like:

Luxury real estate software + AI intelligence

Principles:

1. Extreme clarity

Users always know what to do next.

2. Visual hierarchy

Large headlines

Smaller contextual data

Smart spacing

3. Motion-driven UI

Smooth animations

AI interactions feel alive

4. Black & White Luxury

Minimal palette

Typography becomes the design.

2. Color System (Premium Black & White)

Main palette:

Primary Black: #0A0A0A
Soft Black: #1C1C1C
White: #FFFFFF
Light Gray: #F5F5F5
Neutral Gray: #9CA3AF

Accent colors (very subtle):

Success: #22C55E
Warning: #F59E0B
Alert: #EF4444
AI Accent: #6366F1

Use color only for meaning:

Green → approved tenant
Yellow → missing documents
Red → risk or late rent.

Everything else stays black and white.
