1. Legal Framework (Ontario)

Your AI tool must follow rules under the Landlord and Tenant Board (LTB) and the Residential Tenancies Act.

Key compliance points:

No rental application fees can be charged to applicants in Ontario.

Tenant disputes, evictions, and enforcement go through the LTB tribunal.

Personal data collected in applications must comply with privacy laws like PIPEDA.

Rental units and agreements follow the Ontario standard lease requirements.

So your AI platform must ensure legal compliance + documentation.
