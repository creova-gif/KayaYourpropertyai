Landlord Experience
1.1 Onboarding & Setup

Screens:

Welcome Screen

AI Tour button

CTA: “Get Started”

Company / Personal Profile

Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Role (Admin, Manager)

AI prompt: auto-fill company details if available

Property & Unit Setup

Add property: address → AI autofill (type, rooms, amenities)

Add units: bedrooms, bathrooms, rent, photos

Optional: co-signer requirements for student tenants

Branding & Customization (for multi-property)

Logo upload, primary/secondary colors, fonts

AI previews branded dashboard

AI Prompts:

AI Property Info Autofill:
Input: Property address, postal code
Output: Property type, rooms, amenities, default rent range

AI Dashboard Generation:
Input: Properties & units
Output: Customized dashboard layout with modules: occupancy, applications, payments, AI insights
1.2 Dashboard Overview

Modules:

Property cards (Occupancy, Rent Collected, AI Risk Score)

Application Cards (Tenant Name, AI Score, Fraud Flags)

Payment Timeline (Past/Upcoming/Overdue)

Maintenance Board (Open/Resolved/High Priority)

AI Assistant Sidebar

Interactions:

Click property → open property dashboard

Click tenant → open tenant card with documents and AI summary

Swipe cards to approve/reject applications

AI Prompts:

AI Tenant Risk Analyzer:
Input: Tenant documents, income, credit, rental history
Output: Risk score (0-100), summary, flags for co-signers or students

AI Bulk Applicant Ranking:
Input: Applications across multiple properties
Output: Sorted list by AI score with color-coded risk (Green/Yellow/Red)

Applications List

Color-coded AI risk badges

Tenant Card

Photos, documents, AI risk summary, income-to-rent ratio, references

Buttons: Approve / Request Info / Reject

AI Summary Panel

Fraud detection

Missing docs flagged

Co-signer verification

AI Prompts:

AI Document Validation:
Input: Tenant uploaded ID, pay stubs, bank statements
Output: Verification badge (Verified/Pending/Fraud Risk)

AI Feedback to Tenant:
Input: Application status & AI analysis
Output: Personalized guidance on missing info or documents
1.4 Lease Generation & Signing

AI Compliance Check (Ontario Residential Tenancies Act)

AI Prompts:

AI Lease Generator:
Input: Tenant info, unit info, rent terms, co-signer info
Output: Ontario-compliant digital lease ready for signature

AI Compliance Checker:
Input: Generated lease
Output: Flags non-compliance, suggests corrections
