Problems We’re Solving
For Landlords

Tenant Screening is Slow and Risky

Verifying documents, checking references, and credit manually takes hours/days.

Managing Multiple Properties

Commercial or rental portfolios can have dozens or hundreds of units.

Document Chaos

Hard to track leases, receipts, notices, or maintenance requests.

Compliance Worries

Ontario rules for notices, rent, evictions — mistakes are costly.

Student Rentals / Short-Term Rentals

High turnover, frequent applications, special considerations (co-signers, guarantors).

For Tenants

Lack of Transparency

Don’t know if their application is approved, or why.

Paperwork Hassles

Leases, notices, receipts — often lost or hard to access.

Payment Tracking

Rent schedules, reminders, receipts.

Limited Communication

Hard to contact landlords or track maintenance requests.

2️⃣ Core Features for Both Audiences
For Landlords

AI Tenant Screening — checks documents, credit, references, risk score.

Multi-Property Dashboard — quick view of all units, occupancy, late payments, maintenance issues.

Document Management — leases, notices, receipts, contracts, and audit logs.

Automated Notices — rent reminders, N4/N5 notices, renewals.

Payment Management — track rent, late fees, generate receipts, integrate with Stripe/Moneris.

Analytics & AI Insights — predictive AI for late payments, churn risk, high-risk tenants.

Custom Workflows — approvals, co-signers, guarantors for students or short-term rentals.

Tenant Communication Hub — one place to send messages, upload requests, confirm approvals.

For Tenants

Tenant Portal — view lease, receipts, notices, payment history.

Document Access — digital lease, signed agreements, application history.

Payment Tracking — schedule, reminders, auto-pay, receipts.

AI Assistance — answers questions about lease terms, payment, or rules.

Application Status — real-time updates on their rental application.

Here’s a seamless UX journey for both landlords and tenants:

1️⃣ Onboarding & Property Setup

Add company or personal profile

Add properties/units

Upload branding if multi-property

AI pre-fills property info (address, type, amenities)

2️⃣ List Property & Share Application Link

Generate smart listing description with AI

Share link with tenants (students or general)

3️⃣ Tenant Application Collection

All applications collected in one portal

AI auto-verifies documents, flags fraud, assigns risk score

4️⃣ Review & Approval

Dashboard shows ranked applicants

AI summaries highlight strengths, risks, co-signers

One-click Approve/Reject

5️⃣ Lease Generation & Digital Signing

AI generates Ontario-compliant lease

Custom rules for students (co-signers, guarantors)

Digital signature collected

6️⃣ Payments & Receipts

Rent tracking for all units

Late fees auto-calculated

AI generates receipts automatically

7️⃣ Ongoing Management

Maintenance requests tracked

Notices generated automatically (N4/N5/N12)

AI predictive alerts (high-risk tenants, late payments)

Analytics & reports for occupancy, revenue, and risks

4️⃣ Payments & Receipts

Lease, notices, receipts, and agreements all in one place

4️⃣ Design Principles for Both Audiences

Transparency & Trust

Both sides always know status, history, and documents

Automation, Not Complexity

AI handles verification, reminders, documents, notices

Landlords and tenants interact with clean, visual summaries

Customizable UI

Multi-property companies: branded dashboards

Students: simplified app-like interface

Families: focus on document clarity and payment tracking

Mobile-First Experience

Most students and landlords prefer mobile

Swipe actions for quick approvals

Notifications for updates & reminders
