1. Core Problems Landlords Face
Problem 1: Slow Tenant Screening

Landlords receive many applications but must manually:

read emails

download documents

check credit

call references

Typical process:

Application received
↓
Email back and forth
↓
Download documents
↓
Verify income
↓
Call employer
↓
Call landlord
↓
Make decision

Time: 1–3 days

Solution

AI automates screening.

UX Goal:
Application → Decision in minutes

Problem 2: Fake Documents & Fraud

Common issues:

fake pay stubs

fake employer letters

identity fraud

edited bank statements

Small landlords cannot easily detect fraud.

Solution

AI document verification.

UX:

Upload pay stub
↓
AI scans document
↓
Fraud detection
↓
Verification badge

Example output:

✔ Verified Employer
✔ Verified Income
⚠ Suspicious formatting detected
Problem 3: Too Many Applications

Landlords can receive 50+ applications.

Reviewing them manually is overwhelming.

Solution

AI ranking system.

UX:

Applicants ranked automatically

#1 Sarah Kim
Score: 91

#2 Michael Patel
Score: 84

#3 Jason Lee
Score: 73

Landlord reviews the best candidates first.

Problem 4: Legal Complexity (Ontario)

Landlords often ask:

Can I increase rent?

What notice form do I use?

When can I evict?

Rules come from the Landlord and Tenant Board under the Residential Tenancies Act.

Mistakes can cost landlords thousands.

Solution

AI legal assistant.

UX:

Ask AI

"Can I raise rent?"

AI explains rules
and generates proper notice forms.

3. Key UX Principles for the Platform
1. Zero Cognitive Load

No complicated forms.

Use:

cards

checklists

progress bars

2. AI Does the Heavy Work

Users should not analyze documents manually.

AI summarizes everything.

Example:

AI Summary

Stable employment
High income
Strong rental history
No fraud signals
3. Visual Decision Making

Use visual signals:

Green = safe
Yellow = caution
Red = risk

Example:

Income Stability
🟢 High
4. One-Click Actions

Landlords should never feel stuck.

Actions:

Approve
Request Info
Reject
4. Emotional UX (Very Important)
