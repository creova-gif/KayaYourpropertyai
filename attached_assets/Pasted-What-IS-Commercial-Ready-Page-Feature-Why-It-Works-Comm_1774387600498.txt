Page / Feature	Why It Works

What is RESIDENTIAL-ONLY ❌
Page / Feature	The Problem
Properties.tsx	Hardcoded model: bedrooms, bathrooms, unit number, monthly rent. No sqft, no CAM, no lease type. Adding a commercial property here is impossible
Property Onboarding Wizard	Only residential types: Apartment, Condo, House, Duplex, Townhouse. Wired directly to Ontario Standard Lease (RTA) — commercial leases never use this
List Property (/app/list-property)	Same issue — only Condo, Apartment, House selectable. No commercial option
LTB Workflow	100% residential. The Landlord and Tenant Board in Ontario only handles residential tenancies. Commercial disputes go to Ontario Superior Court or arbitration — completely different process
Compliance Centre	All items reference RTA, LTB, N-forms. Zero commercial compliance coverage (no REBBA, no OREA, no commercial lease law, no HST registrant obligations)
Province Lease Templates	Only Ontario Standard Lease (residential) + provincial residential templates. Commercial leases are freeform contracts, not standard forms
T776 Tax Report	CRA T776 = "Statement of Real Estate Rentals" — this is personal rental income (residential). Commercial properties file differently: HST is charged/collected, CCA classes differ, corporation vs. personal filing matters
HST/GST Tracker	References T776 and residential CRA lines. Commercial real estate is a taxable supply — the HST rules are completely different (input tax credits, self-supply rules, etc.)
Tenant Screening / Applications	Screens individual people (credit score, employment, eviction history). Cannot screen a business entity (corporate credit, CBCA incorporation docs, financial statements, personal guarantees)
Rent Collection	Built around individual rent payments via Interac e-Transfer / Stripe. No CAM reconciliation, no NNN adjustments, no holdover rent, no base + variable rent structures
Financial Dashboard	Shows rent income as a flat number. No CAM breakdown, no lease type differentiation, no gross-to-net calculation for commercial portfolios
Dashboard (main)	Generates N4 notices for units — a residential-only LTB form. Shows bedroom counts
Vacancy Marketing (new)	Uses beds/baths model for vacant units. No commercial sqft / price-per-sqft / lease type framing
Owner Portal (new)	Shows residential net income model. No CAM, NNN, or commercial lease breakdowns
Property Inspection (new)	Checklist items are residential (kitchen appliances, bathroom fixtures, etc.) — doesn't reflect commercial inspection needs (HVAC systems, electrical panels, loading docks, fire suppression, ADA compliance)
What's Completely Missing for Commercial ❌❌
These things don't exist anywhere in the app:

Missing Feature	Why Commercial Needs It
Business tenant screening	Companies need corporate credit checks, articles of incorporation, financial statements, personal guarantees from principals — not individual Equifax scores
Letter of Intent (LOI) generator	Standard first step in commercial leasing before a formal lease
Commercial lease builder	Freeform negotiated contracts, not standard forms. Includes exclusivity clauses, tenant improvement allowances, rent-free periods, renewal options
CAM reconciliation	Annual true-up of estimated vs. actual CAM charges — every NNN lease requires this
Commercial compliance	REBBA 2002, OREA forms, Ontario Business Corporations Act, HST registrant rules, ESA commercial disclosure
Corporate owner/entity management	Commercial properties are held in corporations or partnerships — the app only knows individual landlords
Lease abstraction	Large portfolios need key dates (rent steps, option deadlines, termination rights) extracted from lease documents
