## 🔄 Complete Workflows

### **1. Tenant Application Journey**

```
1. Browse Listings (/listings)
   ↓
2. Submit Application (/tenant/application)
   • 5-step form
   • Document upload
   • Auto-save progress
   ↓
3. AI Processing
   • Document verification
   • Risk analysis (0-100 score)
   • Income verification
   ↓
4. Landlord Review (/colorful/applications)
   • Timeline view
   • AI recommendations
   • One-click approve/reject
   ↓
5. If Approved:
   • Unit auto-reserved
   • AI generates lease
   • Tenant notified
   • Digital signing initiated
   ↓
6. Lease Signing (/tenant/lease/:id)
   • Section-by-section review
   • Digital signature
   • PDF generated
   ↓
7. Move-In
   • Tenant portal access
   • Payment schedule created
   • Welcome message sent
```

```

   • Property details
   • Unit configuration

   • Amenities
   ↓
2. Publish Listing
   • AI suggests rent price
   • Market analysis
   • Public listing created
   ↓
3. Receive Applications
   • AI pre-screens
   • Risk scores assigned
   • Documents verified
   ↓
4. Review & Approve
   • Timeline view
   • AI insights
   • Compare applicants
   ↓
5. Lease Management
   • AI-generated lease
   • Digital signatures
   • Expiry tracking
   ↓
6. Ongoing Management
   • Rent collection
   • Maintenance tracking
   • Communication
   ↓
7. Analytics
   • Financial dashboard
   • Occupancy metrics
   • AI property analysis
```

### **3. Financial Management Workflow**

